# Draftly Project Guide

## What This Project Is
This is a Django-based content publishing platform with post management, authentication, search, and reader comments.

## Core Business Outcome
Help creators and small brands publish content consistently and manage articles in a structured workflow.

## Who It Is For
- Personal brands and creators
- Small business websites
- Coaches/consultants who need a simple CMS-like blog

## Main Features
- Post model with draft/published status
- Slug-based post URLs
- Post listing with pagination
- Search across title and content
- Auth-protected post create/update/delete
- Author-level edit/delete permissions
- Reader comments on posts
- User registration and login flow

## Why This Matters to Clients
- Lets teams publish and manage content without technical friction
- Supports basic content marketing efforts
- Creates a centralized and organized article workflow

## Offer Positioning
"I help personal brands and small businesses publish content consistently using a custom Django blog platform with role-aware workflows."
